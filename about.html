About UKAF Polymer

UKAF Polymer is a UK-based supplier of high-quality plastic raw materials and additives, serving clients mainly in the plastics industry. We source our products from trusted partners in the United States, Israel, and Turkey, ensuring consistent quality and reliable supply for our customers.

With over 20 years of technical experience, we go beyond distribution. Our team provides specialized product and process consultancy, helping plastic manufacturers enhance performance, reduce costs, and optimize their formulations.

At UKAF Polymer, our focus is clear: to deliver flawless operations, customer-oriented service, and long-term value.

As we continue to expand our reach to African markets, we remain committed to being a trusted global partner in the polymer supply chain.
